Whoosh: Инновации в индонезийския високоскоростен транспорт

Whoosh is Southeast Asia's first high-speed train, officially launched in Indonesia on October 2, 2023. Designed to connect Jakarta and Bandung, this train can travel at speeds of up to 350 km/h, significantly reducing the journey time from around three hours to just 45 minutes. With a capacity for 600 passengers, Whoosh exemplifies modern transport efficiency, seamlessly integrating with other transport systems. café on board. The service reflects Indonesia's commitment to modernization in public transportation, showcasing state-of-the-art technology and design.
