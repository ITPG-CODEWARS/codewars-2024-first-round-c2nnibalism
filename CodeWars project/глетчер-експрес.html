Glacier Express: Пътуване през швейцарските алпи

The Glacier Express is renowned as the "slowest express train in the world," offering a breathtaking journey through the Swiss Alps. This iconic train travels between Zermatt and St. Moritz, covering a distance of approximately 291 kilometers in about eight hours. It passes through an impressive 91 tunnels and over 291 bridges, providing passengers with stunning views of the alpine landscape. The Glacier Express features large panoramic windows that enhance the visual experience, allowing travelers to fully immerse themselves in the picturesque scenery. In addition to its scenic beauty, the train offers luxurious amenities, particularly in its Excellence Class, where guests enjoy gourmet meals and personalized service while traveling through this magnificent region.
